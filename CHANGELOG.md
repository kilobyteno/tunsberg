## 0.2.0 - 2025-10-16

### What's Changed

* Introduced a new log_config function to replace uvicorn_log_config, supporting more flexible handler and formatter options, including a custom JSON formatter.
* ⚠️ Marked uvicorn_log_config as deprecated.
